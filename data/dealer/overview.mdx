<p class='font-medium'>
  Our dealer API grants users access to an extensive database of comprehensive vehicle information,
  encompassing detailed specifications and pricing data. With the API capabilities, you can retrieve
  various details about dealers, such as name, address, phone number, and website. Whether you need
  to retrieve dealer specifications or pricing information, our API has you covered.
</p>

## API Capabilities

- **Dealer Information**: Retrieve detailed dealer information, including name, address, phone number, and website.
- **Dealer Contacts**: Lookup dealer contacts, including name, title, phone number, and email address.
- **Dealer Pricing**: Retrieve dealer pricing information, including MSRP, invoice price, and current market price.
