<p class='font-medium'>
  The Back Office & Contracting API from Drivly is engineered to facilitate the smooth handling of
  critical administrative tasks such as notarization and power of attorney issuance. This tool is
  essential for businesses looking to streamline their back-office operations, ensuring legal and
  procedural compliance while enhancing efficiency and client service levels. With this API,
  automotive businesses can handle legal documentation and authorizations more effectively,
  supporting a more streamlined and customer-friendly transaction process.
</p>

## API Capabilities

- **Notarization**: Notarize documents for your customers.
- **Power of Attorney**: Issue power of attorney for your customers.
