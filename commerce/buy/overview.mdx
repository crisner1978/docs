- **Vehicle Search**: Find vehicles based on make, model, year, price, and location.
- **Vehicle Information**: Access detailed vehicle information, including images, descriptions, and pricing.
- **Purchase Completion**: Securely complete purchases with seller support.
- **Seller Communication**: Communicate directly with sellers to ask questions and negotiate terms.
